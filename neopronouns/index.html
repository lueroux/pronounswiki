Everything You Need to Know About Neo-Pronouns

Neopronouns (or neo-pronouns) encompass any pronouns beyond the traditional sets of “he/him,” “she/her,” and “they/them.” Individuals may choose neopronouns because these forms feel more accurate or comfortable for their sense of self. The sets below illustrate the wide range of neopronouns currently in use. As language and identities evolve, so do neopronouns; this list represents a snapshot rather than a fixed inventory.

You can learn more about Neopronouns here.
